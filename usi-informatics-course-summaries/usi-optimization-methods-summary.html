Course overview

Theory and practice of deterministic optimisation: from first-order methods to simplex and KKT conditions.

Main topics

  • Problem taxonomy (linear vs non-linear, convexity, continuous/discrete)
  • Unconstrained optimisation — gradient descent, Newton & quasi-Newton
  • Line-search strategies (Wolfe, backtracking) & convergence analysis
  • Constrained optimisation (active sets, first/second-order conditions)
  • Linear programming — geometry of polyhedra & the simplex algorithm
