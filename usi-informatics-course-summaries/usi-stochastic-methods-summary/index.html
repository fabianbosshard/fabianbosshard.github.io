Course overview

A first course in probability, Monte-Carlo simulation and stochastic processes with an eye toward data science applications.

Main topics

  • Random numbers & inverse-transform sampling basics
  • Probability measures, random variables, expectation & variance
  • Law of Large Numbers & Central Limit Theorem with illustrations
  • Monte-Carlo methods — importance & rejection sampling, variance reduction
  • Markov chains, Poisson processes & stochastic differential equations
